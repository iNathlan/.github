## ✦ WHO WE ARE ✦

<div align="center">

Nathlan Solutions is a technology company driven by innovation. We create cutting-edge software and proprietary technologies that redefine how humans interact with sound, artificial intelligence, and digital systems. Our mission is to push the boundaries of what's possible in audio synthesis, machine learning, and open-source tooling for creative and technical industries.

**Companies:** [Akadion](https://github.com/akadion) | [DevStack](https://github.com/iakadion) | [Void Sessions](https://github.com/iakadion)
